Non-disclosure agreements

Introduction

As part of your participation in this program, you'll be asked to sign a non-disclosure agreement. There are important things you need to know about this agreement. In addition to reading the document itself carefully to understand its specific terms, review the materials on this page carefully to learn about NDA's in general. You'll be asked to confirm your completion of the material at the end of the page.

Adhering to your NDA

Sticking with your NDA might be simple: just don't talk about it. It's not always so simple, however, and you may encounter situations where there's pressure to share information to break your NDA, or where you're discussing information that breaks your NDA but it isn't obvious.
